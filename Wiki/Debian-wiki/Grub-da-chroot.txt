
** Montare la partizione sulla quale risiede il sistema


     mount /dev/sda3 /mnt
     mount --bind /dev /mnt/dev
     mount --bind /proc /mnt/proc
     mount --bind /sys /mnt/sys

     chroot /mnt

     grub-install /dev/sda
     update-grub2



** Uscire dal chroot e Smontare tutti i dispositivi:

     exit
     cd ~
     umount /mnt/dev
     umount /mnt/proc
     umount /mnt/sys
     umount /mnt/

     reboot
